WeCare

WeCare is an NGO working for specially-abled people(both adults and children). We help people with visual,cognitive, and physical challenges. We aim to provide them a bright future.

WHO WE ARE ↥

We're a group of enthusiastic volunteers willing to bring about
a positive change in the society. We are people
from varied professions and sections of the society willing to
do our bit for the society.

WHAT WE DO ↥

We run 2 schools for special children. We organize events
round the year, which feature performances of special children.
We also organize interaction events between special children and couselors.
